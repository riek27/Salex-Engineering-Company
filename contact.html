Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically take 8-12 weeks, while commercial projects may take 12-20 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you work with remote clients?

Yes! We regularly work with clients across the country through virtual consultations, 3D renderings, and detailed project management tools to ensure a seamless experience regardless of location.

What is your design fee structure?

We offer flexible pricing options including flat fee, hourly rate, or percentage of project cost. During our initial consultation, we'll recommend the best approach for your specific project needs and budget.

Do you handle project management?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive project management services, coordinating with contractors, suppliers, and artisans to ensure your project stays on schedule and within budget.

Can you work within my budget?

Yes, we're experienced at creating beautiful designs within various budget ranges. We'll be transparent about costs and help prioritize elements to maximize your investment.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out our contact form or call us to schedule a complimentary initial consultation. We'll discuss your vision, requirements, and timeline to determine if we're the right fit for your project.
